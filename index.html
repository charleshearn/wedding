Hey look, we love each other!

Dorothy and Charles met in Brooklyn, NY in April 2017 after a couple of weeks trying to coordinate their first date over Hinge. Dorothy suggested her favorite whiskey bar, Noorman's Kil (RIP). Subsequent early dates included Charles introducing Dorothy to Ash (their dog) and a plant repotting date to Home Depot. From there, it's been two Brooklyn apartments, many homemade cheese plates (#cheesewithcharles), memorable travels, countless dog walks, and a global pandemic (NBD). Nothing like an unprecedented health crisis to make it clear who you're meant to spend your life with.

We got engaged on July 11th, 2020 on Rough Ridge off the Blue Ridge Parkway.

Charles packed apples. A kind hiker took this photo for us.

Our wedding will be on Saturday, October 16th in Morganton, NC, Dorothy's hometown.

We are so excited to celebrate with you and hope you will make wonderful memories in the foothills with us! It will also be peak leaf season, which means you must go on a hike or at least a drive up the Blue Ridge Parkway.

Crazy times!

It goes without saying, but nothing is more important to us than the health and safety of our friends and loved ones. We respectfully ask that anyone joining us to celebrate be fully vaccinated against COVID-19.

Morganton, North Carolina

Morganton is at the foothills of the Blue Ridge mountains about 1.5 hours northwest of Charlotte, NC; an hour north of Greenville, SC; and about an hour east of Asheville, NC. The Catawba River runs through it and will be the backdrop of our wedding ceremony and reception. Check out the FAQs page for some of our favorite Morganton spots!

Getting there

Please check out our FAQs page for more details on how to get to Morganton, including airport recommendations.

We are happy to try connecting you with other guests who may be renting cars and open to carpooling. Please keep in mind that options for Uber, Lyft, and other ride sharing are limited, so it is helpful to have access to a car if you're interested in hiking or other activities outside of the scheduled wedding events.
