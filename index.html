Morganton, North Carolina

Morganton is at the foothills of the Blue Ridge mountains about 1.5 hours northwest of Charlotte, NC; an hour north of Greenville, SC; and about an hour east of Asheville, NC. The Catawba River runs through it and will be the backdrop of our wedding ceremony and reception.

Getting there

If you're flying, we recommend flying into Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT) or Greenville-Spartanburg International Airport (GSP). While Asheville (AVL) is closer, it's a regional airport so there are less options and they tend to be more expensive. Other NC airports in Greensboro (GSO) and in the Triangle area (RDU) would be a longer drive, but may have good options depending on where you're coming from!

We do recommend renting a car at the airport or carpooling, if possible. There is a shuttle service, the Hickory Hop, that runs between the Charlotte, Asheville, and Hickory airports and will get you close to Morganton, but you would need a rental car or a ride from Hickory (approx. 30 minutes). Once we get closer to our wedding date, we are happy to try connecting you with other guests who may be carpooling.

Please keep in mind that Morganton options for Uber, Lyft, and other ride sharing are limited and pretty hit-or-miss, so it is helpful to have access to a car if you're interested in hiking or doing other activities outside of the scheduled wedding events. We will have shuttle transportation arranged from the two hotels with room blocks to and from the wedding on Saturday.
